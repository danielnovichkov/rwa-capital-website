How it works

1

AI‑powered asset analysis

We extract key data from documents, screen legal and operational risks, and compile an asset dossier. Predictive models assess returns and scenarios.

2

Registration and compliance

An AI assistant guides investor profiling and documentation. KYC/AML is performed by independent, regulated providers.

3

Transactions and custody

Token purchase, regulated custody, and secondary market access are facilitated through authorized third‑party providers. RWA Capital does not hold client funds.
